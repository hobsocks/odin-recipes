Apple Pie Recipe

Description

This amazing apple pie recipe will be a smash hit every Thanksgiving, and was a recipe that was personally handed down to me by my grandmother! She always made the best apple pie, and now I'm going to share it all with you!

  • 8 small Granny Smith apples
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 (9 inch) double-crust pie pastry, thawed
