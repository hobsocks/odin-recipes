Fried Chicken Recipe

Description

A southern classic! This crispy fried chicken is way healthier than what you would find at your local fried chicken shop, and uses fresher ingredients to boot!

  • 1 (4 pound) chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour for coating
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 quarts vegetable oil for frying
