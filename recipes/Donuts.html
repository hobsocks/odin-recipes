Donut Recipe

Description

Skip out on making the trip to the donut shop with this glazed donut recipe! Perfect to have alongside a cup of coffee, these donuts are sure to put a smile on your face!

  • 2 (.25 ounce) envelopes active dry yeast
  • ¼ cup warm water (105 to 115 degrees)
  • 1 ½ cups lukewarm milk
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • ⅓ cup shortening
  • 5 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 quart vegetable oil for frying
  • ⅓ cup butter
  • 2 cups confectioners' sugar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons vanilla
  • 4 tablespoons hot water or as needed
